Services

Glassblowing Lessons

Come by over and learn how to work with hot glass straight out the furnace! We’ll cover safety and techniques appropriate based off of level. Single-person and Group options are available. You can be assured that by the end of the lesson you’ll be able to make a piece you’ll enjoy having! We’ll make contact with you after to work a time for piece pickup.

Glass Project Consulting

Do you have an idea as to what you’re looking to make? Unsure of the feasibility of the project and need a second opinion? Look no further! We’ll be sure to work with you through discussions and meetings, as well as co-ordinate proof of concepts to help you make your idea come to life!

Studio Glassware to Sell

We also have our own glassware made here at the studio. The Shop page will be made soon, but check it out to see about buying some of our wonderful pieces from the studio!
